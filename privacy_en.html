Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2026
Effective Date: March 27, 2026

Welcome to PicMore ("the App" or "we"). We understand the importance of personal information to you and will do our best to protect your personal information in a safe and reliable manner. This Privacy Policy is intended to help you understand what data we collect, why we collect it, what we do with it, and how we protect it.

1. How We Collect and Use Your Information

In order to provide you with core functions such as image editing, caption stitching, and adding watermarks, we may need to collect and use the following information:

  • Album and Storage: To use "Caption Stitching" and "Album Watermark", we access your album. All processing is performed locally and your photos are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Camera: For the "Watermark Camera", we access your device camera. Photos are saved and processed only on your device.
  • Location: To display location info on watermarks, we collect location data only after your explicit authorization.
  • Device Info: We may collect non-identifiable data (device model, OS version, crash logs) to ensure app stability and optimize experience.

2. Localized Processing Statement

We take your privacy seriously. All core image processing (cropping, watermarking, stitching) is performed completely locally on your device. We do NOT upload, store, or share your original or processed images with any cloud servers.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share personal information with third parties except with your consent or as required by law. The App integrates standard SDKs (like Google Play Billing) which collect necessary data per their own policies.

4. Your Rights

You can manage permissions at any time via system settings. Revoking core permissions (camera/storage) may limit app functionality.

5. Minors

This App is intended for adults. Minors should use it under parental or guardian supervision.

6. Policy Updates

We may revise this policy. Material changes will be notified via in-app prompts. Changes are effective upon publication.

7. Contact Us

For any questions regarding privacy, please contact us via the developer email provided in the App.
